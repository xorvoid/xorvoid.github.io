Calico Basin Scrambling

The original goal was to rappel off the top of Moderate Mecca. But, I decided instead to make it an improvised scramble/traverse across the entire area.

I really liked this spot. Very secluded. Lots of hollow & loose rock suggests it has rarely been visited.
