Forty Months in Exile

It's been forty months in exile. Often, wandering the desert. And, the old story holds up.

What have I learned? Much.

How has my world view changed? Completely.

What can I say about it? Almost nothing.

The story begins much earlier. It begins with tragedy. It begins with unraveling. It begins with rebirth and the slow death of before.

I've visited before. But, this trip has been different.

I would like to describe, but words do not posses the capacity. It's an enigma illuminated only through the journey. And the journey is long and besought with peril.

But, I am here now.
